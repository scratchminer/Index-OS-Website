It's like the Launcher you already know, but...better!!

Introducing Index OS, the world-class custom Launcher app built just for Playdate.

We built Index to wring as much sheer customization, personality, and FUN as you can out of a custom Playdate Launcher!

Why choose Index?

Setup is easy as pie.

Get your device running on Index OS, as easy as sideloading a game. Set everything up perfectly straight from your device, with no outside tools necessary.

Sorting? Easy!

Choose between a variety of sorting presets from the slide menu, or pick the games up yourself and shuffle 'em around any way you want!

Custom folders!

Group your games together, any way you want 'em. Make folders with custom names, drag and drop and organize to your heart's content - no extra setup necessary.

Change your mind?

If you ever wanna switch back to the stock Launcher, you can do so as easily as you set it up. Or, you can just take a little vacation if you want. Keep your options open.
